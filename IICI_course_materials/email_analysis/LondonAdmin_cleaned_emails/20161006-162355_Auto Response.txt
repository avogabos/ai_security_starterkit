Thank you for emailing the Northampton County Court, we will reply as soon as possible. Please take the time to read this message as it contains important information.


The Northampton County Court is a separate entity to the Northampton County Court Business Centre (CCBC) and the County Court Money Claim Centre (CCMCC) and enquiries related to cases proceeding in those offices will not be responded to or forwarded.  We do not deal with queries for those offices.  The email addresses for CCBC/CCMCC are:
ccbcdefendants@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk<mailto:ccbcdefendants@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk> or ccbcclaimants@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk<mailto:ccbcclaimants@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk>
ccmcccustomerenquiries@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk<mailto:ccmcccustomerenquiries@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk>

You may receive a direct reply via email or in writing depending on the nature of your query

Attachments must be sent in a format supported by the software used by the specified Court to which it was sent.  The formats are listed on her Majesty's Courts and Tribunals Service website. Attachments sent in a format not listed will be treated as having not been received.

If an email is received after 4pm the email will be treated as filed the next working day.

The Court Office is no longer able to provide forms and you may find the following links useful.

http://www.hmcourts-service.gov.uk/HMCTSCourtFinder

Court forms can be obtained from the HMCTS website (please select 'County Court' from the drop down menu):

http://www.hmcourts-service.gov.uk/HMCTSCourtFinder/FormFinder.do

For information on court fees go to:

http://www.hmcourts-service.gov.uk/infoabout/fees/index.htm

Legal Advice

Court Staff are not legally trained and cannot advise you.
If you are uncertain how to proceed, the Civil Procedure Rules are available on the Ministry of Justice website - http://www.justice.gov.uk<http://www.justice.gov.uk/> . On many occasions it is best for people to seek professional legal advice from a solicitor, legal executive, legal advice agency or Citizens Advice Bureau. You can also contact Community Legal Advice for free legal advice on 0845 345 4 345 or via their website at http://www.communitylegaladvice.org.uk/

For UK: We can certify the docs

 

For Gambia: We can Certify the docs

 

For HK: our notary in HK certifies, charge customer £250 for the docs

 

For Seychelles, BVI, Belize: we will need the agent to send the Apostile

 

3-5 days opening from me sending all docs

 

The following has to be filled and given to me,

 

I will need the following ID docs, they need to be clear scans, I do not
need the originals:

Passport  Notarised

Proof of Address (preferably Bank Statement)  Notarised

 

2 Bank Statements (one can be as above  if recent) and the 2nd should be
about 5-6 months old

 

If existing company over 6 months then additionally:

COGS & Incumbency

Any previous bank statements

 

The following links should be filled as much as possible, or info given to
me so I can fill and send.

https://files.europacbank.com/docs/account_preapproval_corporate.pdf

http://files.europacbank.com/docs/EPB_contract_corporate.pdf

 

https://files.europacbank.com/docs/EPB_corporate_resolution_template.doc

 

do the 1st one and send me to get pre approval, then fill 2nd 2 and get
signed.

 

Once the account is opened, the customer can login and then make an
activation payment of $500. After this they are able to order a debit card.
The debit card services are very similar to FBME in that its a pre loaded
card etc.

 

The only additional rule is NO MONEY IN OR OUT OF RUSSIA and of course the
nationality rules in the link

http://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/sanctions/Programs/Pages/Programs.as
px

 

Any questions let me know

Thank you for your e mail, if the company is being restored to remain on the

you may apply for administrative restoration, we would require the following
documents and fees: 

RT01 form and application fee of £100 

The Bona Vacantia waiver letter, details can be found on the website gov.uk 
please enter waiver letter in the search box for further information. 

Outstanding late filing penalties 

Accounts for period end 31/05/08 £375 
Accounts for period end 31/05/10 £750 
Accounts for period end 31/05/11 £1500 

Annual Accounts for period end 31/05/14 and late filing  penalty of £1500 

Annual Returns for period end 29/11/10 and filing fee of £40. 
Annual Returns for period end 26/12/14 and filing fee of £40. 

The Annual Returns forms can be downloaded from the website.gov.uk please
enter AR01 in the search box 
and follow the link for file your annual return for the most recent form. 

The company was dissolved as the accounts for 31/05/14, and the annual
return for 26/12/14 were not filed. 

We must receive an application within six years of the dissolved date. 


We cannot accept a cheque drawn on the company account, as when a company 
is dissolved, the company bank account is frozen. 

Please allow ten working days from the day of receipt for us to process the
documents. 

All the required documents and fees should be sent in together, and marked
for the attention of the Restoration Section (RT01).

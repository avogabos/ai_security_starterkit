Thank you for your email.

Please assist us in finalizing your company registration by providing us with the following identification documents for the company director/s & shareholders:

1. Proof of ID (copy of passport)
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (issue date, name, address and account number must be visible). 

If above documents are not in English then we will need officially translated notarized copies of documents with the originals. The above items can be sent as a scanned copy by email.

Also, please provide us the following details:

-Brief description of business activity
-Specify the PSC officer. All UK companies need to keep a register of people with significant control ('PSC register'). Companies registered from 30th June 2016 should provide PSC details (Person with significant control), person who holds ownership and has a right to take any action or to make any changes for e.g.: appointment, termination etc. The secretary or director can be appointed as PSC officer.

MARKETING CONSULTANTS INTERNATIONAL LIMITED        Dec 19, 2017

It is a mandatory requirement for a company to file its Annual Return with Companies House. An Annual Return is a snapshot of general information about a company's Director(s) and Secretary, registered office address, shareholders and share capital. If the Annual Returns are filed late, or not at all, the Company and its Director(s) and Secretary are liable for prosecution.

To help you in quick and efficient Annual Return (and other relevant) filing(s), Formations House offers an Online Company Administrator service. Online Company Administrator can help you file your Annual Return for your company within minutes. Subscribers to the Online Company Administrator can simply go to the Formation House website www.formationshouse.com<http://www.formationshouse.com/> and log on to the Online Company Administrator to file Annual Return for their company. To subscribe to the Online Company Administrator, please log on to your account at the Formations House website and click on the Upgrade button next to the Online Company Administrator option.

If you have any further queries, please contact our support department at: support@formationshouse.com<mailto:support@formationshouse.com>

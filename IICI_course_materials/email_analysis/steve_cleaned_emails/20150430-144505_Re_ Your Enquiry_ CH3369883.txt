Dear Madam
Thank-you for your email regarding 07541976 FIRST EUROPEAN SOLUTIONS LTD

To restore the company by Administrative Restoration, the following paper documents and fees are required:-

1)  RT01 form, with a filing fee of £100.

2)  B.V. Waiver Letter which costs £64 can be obtained from the Government Legal Department Office   (apply online at www.gov.uk/bonavacantia)

3) Annual Return form for the year ending 16/5/14 and £40 filing fee. (The Annual Return form for the year ending 16/5/15 and £40 filing fee due for filing by 13/6/15.)

There are no late filing penalties. The company was remove for non-compliance.

regards

Mr R. Davies
Restoration Department

    Bank Contract      
     
Business summary does not correspond to that which is provided on the account pre-approval form   
  
Relevant details not visible due to poor quality photocopy   
  
Fields contain incomplete answers or remain empty   
  
Registered corporate address doesn't match that which is on the Memorandum of Association   
  
The client's signature doesn't match that which is on the client's passport or other photo ID   
  
The client's full name and/or date of signature are omitted

Registered corporate address doesn't match that which is on the Memorandum of Association   
  
Relevant details not visible due to poor quality photocopy   
  
Name(s) of the individual(s) authorized to operate the account are missing or not all present   
  
The Director's signature doesn't match that which is on the Director's passport or other photo ID

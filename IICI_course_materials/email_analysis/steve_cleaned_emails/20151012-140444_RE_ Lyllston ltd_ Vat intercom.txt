Hello Eric

 

We have checked and company name VAT INTERCOM is provisionally available for
registration in UK.

 

To place UK company formation order for VAT INTERCOM, please go to
http://www.formationshouse.com/company_formation.htm and click on Order
Here button. Complete the online ordering procedure by providing company
name to be registered, selecting additional services, providing information
on director and shareholder to be appointed and payment details. You may
need following additional services:

 

-         Registered Address

-         Director Privacy/Protection Service

-         Apostille set of company documents

-         Online Company Administrator

-         Value Accounting

 

Once we have your order, payment and due diligence documents, company will
be registered within 3 working days.

 

 

Documents required for due diligence for company formation:

 

Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial
owner/director/s and shareholder/s.

 

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport

2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas
OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (Please do
not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)

 

If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated
and notorized. And send original and translated copies.

 

Regards

 

Steve

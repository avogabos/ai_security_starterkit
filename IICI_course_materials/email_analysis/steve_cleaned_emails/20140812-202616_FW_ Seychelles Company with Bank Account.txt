Documents required for due diligence for company formation:


Scan copies of following documents are required for company owner/director/s.


If company director a Natural Person:

1.      Proof of ID - Valid Passport (Notarized within last 3 months)



2.      Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (Notarized within last 3 months)

 

If company director a Corporate Entity:

1.      Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles) (Notarized within last 3 months)
. 

2.      Proof of ID and Proof of Address of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body (Notarized within last 3 months)

 

If documents are not in English, then you are required to have them translated and notarized.

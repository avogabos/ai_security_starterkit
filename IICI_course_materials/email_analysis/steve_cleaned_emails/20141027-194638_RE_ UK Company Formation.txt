Company Registration, please fill the form and send it back to me. The cost
for UK company registration is 50 GBP.

 

You can choose following additional services:

 

-          Registered Address 25 GBP for 1st year (2nd year onwards renewal
100 GBP per annum)

-          Natural Person Nominee Director 500 GBP per annum

-          Nominee Shareholder 25 GBP

-          Apostille set of company documents 300 GBP

-          Virtual Office Mail address 275 GBP per annum

-          Virtual Office Phone and Fax 275 GBP per annum

-          Online Company Administrator 25 GBP per annum (to file Annual
Returns at year end)

-          Value Accounting 300 GBP per annum (to prepare and file Annual
Accounts and Tax Returns at year end)

-          Power of Attorney 300 GBP

 

 

Please note using Nominee Services and Power Attorney, you will not be able
to open bank account for the company, to open bank account you will need to
appoint your own director and shareholder.

 

 

Along with order, please also provide following documents of the person who
will be the beneficial owner and for the person who will be mentioned on
power of attorney.

Hello,


Thank you for your email.


We can certainly assit you in registering following types of companies in Scotland:

- Scotland Private Limited Compay by Shares : GBP 50
- SCOTTISH LP Registration with Registered Address for 1 year : GBP 750


- Scotland Private Limited Compay by Shares : GBP 50:

You need to provide:

Registered Address in Scotland
Minimum 1 human director
Minimum 1 shareholder

Upon receiving the order, payment and due diligence documents, company will be registered within 2 working days.


- SCOTTISH LP Registration with Registered Address for 1 year : GBP 750:

You need to provide minimum 2 partners

Upon receiving the order, payment and due diligence documents, company will be registered within 10 working days.


Documents required for due diligence for company formation:

Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial owner/director/s and shareholder/s. Please make sure that scans are clear and in readable size and format.


If Human Entity:

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature must be visible)
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (date, name, address and account number must be visible)

(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)


If Corporate Entity:

1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles)
2. Proof of ID (Valid Passport) and Proof of Address (Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.) of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body.(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)


If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.


Please advice what type of company you would like to register and we will guide you with the ordering procedure.

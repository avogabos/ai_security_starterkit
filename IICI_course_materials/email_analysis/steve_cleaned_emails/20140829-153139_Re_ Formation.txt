Dear R. R. Arnold


Thank you for your interest in our services.

Following are the details of our UK Company Formation and Bank Account processing service.


UK Company Formation:

The cost to register a private limited company in the UK is 50 GBP; the time required to complete the procedure is 3-4 working days.

However, if you choose our Express formation service for additional 25 GBP, your company will be formed within 8 working hours (our working hours are 0900-1700 GMT).

The company package includes:

- Company formation fee
- Domain name registration
- 1 free email address
- Preparation of Memorandum and Articles
- Hard copy of certificate of incorporation
- Online account to manage your company register (company documents)

The basic company requirements are as follows:

• 1 Company Director (can be of any nationality and can reside anywhere in the world)
• 1 Shareholder (Director can also be a Shareholder)
• Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address in England, Wales or Scotland).


To place an order for company formation, please click on the link http://www.formationshouse.com/company_formation.htm. This link will take you to the 1st step of our 7 step online company formation procedure. On the 2nd step of this procedure you can choose additional services.


Documents required for due diligence:

Scan copies of following documents are required for company owner/director/s.

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.

If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notarized. And send original and translated copies.


Registered address service:

This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you cannot provide avalid UK postal address; the cost for this service is 25 GBP.

Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving official mail coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to the sender.

To place an order for this service, please click on the option available on the 2nd step of online ordering procedure.


Bank Account: (optional)

We provide bank account service with following banks:

- Barclays Bank - UK (for UK companies only, company director/s presence will be required)

- Royal Bank of Scotland - UK (for UK companies only, company director/s presence will be required)

- OCBC Bank. Singapore (Oversea-Chinese Banking Corporation Limited): (Visit required in London Branch)

- Euro Pacific Bank. St. Vincent (Skype meeting with customer directly, no visit required)

- Rietumu Banka. Latvia (For EU base customers visit not required) (For non EU customer visit is required in London)

The bank account opening service cost 500 GBP and takes 4-6 weeks to process after company registration. Please note that we do not guarantee bank account opening, the final decision is of the bank.


If you require further information or assistance you can contact me at info@formationshouse.com<mailto:info@formationshouse.com> or steve@formationshouse.com<mailto:steve@formationshouse.com> or 0044-(0)207-016-2727 Monday to Friday 12pm to 7pm GMT.


Regards

Steve Smith

We provide UK VAT registration service, we
do not provide Intra-Community VAT registration service.
And to apply for UK VAT, company must have
a trading address in UK, though you are using our Registered Address service,
but do not allow you to use our Registered Address as company’s trading
address. Since your company’s actual trading address is outside of UK then you
will need following address services:
-
Value
Accounting: GBP 300 (per annum)
-
OutSource
Accounts Department: GBP 300 (per annum)
-
Virtual
Office InfiniOffice Mail Only: GBP 275 (GBP 225 per annum)
If you wish to go ahead with UK VAT
registration, then please login to your account our website and click on E-VAT
Registration, here you need to fill and finalize the form. Give as much
information as you can and once you finalize the form, we will then review the
form and contact you for any missing or inaccurate information. We will then
update your application and submit to VAT office for processing.

UK Company Formation:
The cost to register a private limited company in the UK is 50
GBP; the time required to complete the procedure is 3-4 working days.
However, if you choose our Express formation service for
additional 25 GBP, your company will be formed within 8 working hours (our
working hours are 0900-1700 GMT).
The company package includes:
- Company formation fee
- Domain name registration
- 1 free email address
- Preparation of Memorandum and Articles
- Hard copy of certificate of incorporation
- Online account to manage your company register (company
documents)
The basic company requirements are as follows:
• 1 Company Director (can be of any nationality and can
reside anywhere in the world)
• 1 Shareholder (Director can also be a Shareholder)
• Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address
in England, Wales or Scotland).
To place an order for company formation, please click on the link
http://www.formationshouse.com/company_formation.htm
. This link will take you
to the 1st step of our 7 step online company formation procedure. On the 2nd
step of this procedure you can choose additional services.
Documents required for due diligence:
Scan copies of following documents are required for company
owner/director/s.
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR
electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them
translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.
Registered address service:
This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you
cannot provide a valid UK postal address; the cost for this service is 25 GBP.
Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving
official mail coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to
the sender.
To place an order for this service, please click on the option
available on the 2nd step of online ordering procedure.

We can have offshore bank accounts for our customers without any
need for them to personally attend the bank procedure.
For UK Bank Account you are now required to attend the bank
account procedure.
In UK we offer bank account with Royal of Scotland or Barclays.
And Offshore bank account is offered with FBME Cyprus.
Once the account is opened, you will be provided with the account
number after which you can apply for Credit card, Debit card, ATM and/or
internet banking services. Bank account can be opened in the following currencies:
Euros
Pound Sterling
US Dollars

•Signed share certificates
If the required documents are not in English, you will need to
have these translated and certified. Proof of residence does not mean that you

world. This proof of residence provides a confirmation of your residential
address.
Please find listed below the relevant information for signatory/s,

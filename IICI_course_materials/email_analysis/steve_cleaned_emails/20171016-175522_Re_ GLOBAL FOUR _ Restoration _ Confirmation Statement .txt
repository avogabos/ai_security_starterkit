To file Conformation Statement you will need Online Company Administrator service GBP 25 (per annum) and using the service you will file Confirmation Statement and GBP 15 filing fee to government.
To order Online Company Administrator service GBP 25 (per annum) please follow the following steps:
1. Login to your account at our website
www.formationshouse.com
using MY ACCOUNT link and provide your Customer ID and Password.
2. Click on "UK Company Admin" link.
3. In case, if you have multiple companies in your account, select the company name for which you wish to purchase the product/service.
4. Click on "To order new products and services click here" link.
5. Select the product/service you wish to purchase.
6. Click "Proceed" and complete the checkout process by providing payment information.
If you require further information or assistance you can contact us by email, by phone or by live chat on our website.

Thank you for your interest in our services.

 

 

In Europe we provide company formation services in UK, following are the
details of our services.

 

 

UK Company Formation:

 

The cost to register a private limited company in the UK is 50 GBP; the time
required to complete the procedure is 3-4 working days.

 

However, if you choose our Express formation service for additional 25 GBP,
your company will be formed within 8 working hours (our working hours are
0900-1700 GMT). 

 

The company package includes:

 

- Company formation fee

- Domain name registration

- 1 free email address

- Preparation of Memorandum and Articles

- Hard copy of certificate of incorporation

- Online account to manage your company register (company documents)

 

The minimum company requirements are as follows:

 

. 1 Human Director (can be of any nationality and can reside anywhere in the
world)

. 1 Shareholder (Director can also be a Shareholder)

. Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address in England,
Wales or Scotland).

 

To place an order for company formation:

 

- Go to http://www.formationshouse.com/ukcompany.php

- Click on 'Order Now' button

- Provide company name to be registered

- Select any additional services if required

- Provide your authorize contact information and to create Customer ID

- Provide information on company director/s and shareholder/s

- Verify given information and provide payment details to complete the
order.

 

 

Once we have the order, payment and due diligence documents, company
registration will complete in 2 working days.

 

 

Documents required for due diligence:

 

Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial
owner/director/s and shareholder/s. Please make sure that scans are clear
and in readable size and format.

 

Human Entity:

 

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature must be visible)

2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas
OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (date, name,
address and account number must be visible)

 

(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not
acceptable.)

 

 

Corporate Entity:

 

1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of
Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles)

2. Proof of ID (Valid Passport) and Proof of Address (Last 3 months utility
bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card
statement.) of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body.(Please do not
send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)

 

If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated
and notorized. And send original and translated copies.

 

 

Registered address service:

 

This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you cannot
provide a valid UK postal address; the cost for this service is 25 GBP.

 

Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving official mail
coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to the sender.


 

To place an order for this service, please click on the option available on
the 2nd step of online ordering procedure.

 

 

Additional Services:

 

- Director Privacy Service (to hide director's residential address in public
records)

- Shareholder Privacy Service (to hide shareholder's residential address in
public records)

- Online Company Administrator (for filing changes and annual return with
company registrar)

- Value Accounting (preparation and filing of annual accounts and tax
return)

- Virtual Office Mailing address, Phone and Fax.

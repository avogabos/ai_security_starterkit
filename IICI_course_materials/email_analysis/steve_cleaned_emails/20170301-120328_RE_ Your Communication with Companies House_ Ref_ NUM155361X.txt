NEPTUNUS OVERSEAS LIMITED - 09090550

 

 

Thank you for your email concerning the restoration of the above company.  The company was struck off and dissolved due to non compliance of  a statutory document. (Annual Return 17/06/2015).

 There are two procedures for restoring a company to the register, which are:

             - An Administrative Restoration (solely for companies that are to remain on the register)

             - Order of Court to restore the company to the register.

 If you wish to Administratively restore the company to the register Companies House will require the following documents, -

  

 1)  RT01 form, with a filing fee of £100.

  

 2)  The Bona Vacantia waiver letter which costs £64, details can be found on the Government Legal Department Bona Vacantia Division website
 www.gov.uk/government/organisations/bona-vacantia

 3)  Accounts for the period ending 30/06/2015

      

Please note the accounts for the period ending 31/06/2016 is due to be filed by 31/03/2017, if the company is not restored by this due date, these accounts will be required to be filed as part of the restoration
  application. 

 

 

4) Annual Return form AR01 for the year ending 17/06/2015 and £40 filing fee

    Annual Return form AR01 for the year ending 17/06/2016 and £40 filing fee

 

 

We cannot accept a cheque drawn on the company account, as when the company is dissolved, the company bank account is frozen. 
 If you wish to pay by credit /debit card please state this in your covering letter when returning the form. We will then contact you by telephone to take the payment details, once all the documents have been received in an acceptable form.

 

All the required documents and fees should be sent to us marked for the attention of the Restoration Section (RT01). 

 If you intend to restore the company by an Order of Court, information can be found on the Government Legal Department Bona Vacantia Division websitewww.gov.uk/government/organisations/treasury-solicitor-s-department please follow the links for publications
  search guide to  company restoration. 

 

The costs are £300 for the Government Legal Department Bona Vacantia Division fee, plus court and legal fees.

 

If the company is being restored only to recover assets, we will not require accounts or annual returns to be filed, there will not be late filing penalties.

 

We will require documents to be filed to show any change of the registered office address, or any changes relating to the officers of the company.

 

The procedure usually six to eight weeks to restore the company, after the company is restored you may release the assets held in the company name.

 

Once the assets are released, we will require a form DS01 and £10 fee to be filed, to begin the process of dissolving the company.

 

If you should require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.

 

 

Yours faithfully

Linda Winters

Restoration Section

 Companies House

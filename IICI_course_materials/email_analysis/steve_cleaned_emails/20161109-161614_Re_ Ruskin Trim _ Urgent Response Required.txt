Yes shareholder can be a corporate, in this case you need to arrange due diligence documents as per following:

 

Director Human Entity:

 

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature and complete information must be visible)

2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (issue date, name, address and account number must be visible).

 

Shareholder Corporate Entity:

 

1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles)

2. Proof of ID (Valid Passport) and Proof of Address (Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.) of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body.

 

If above documents are not in English then we will need officially translated notarized copies of documents with the originals.

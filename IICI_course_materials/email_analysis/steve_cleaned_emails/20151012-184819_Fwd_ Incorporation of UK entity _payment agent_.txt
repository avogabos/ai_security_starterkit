Dear Bartosz Bialkowski
Thank you for your email.
We can certainly assist you in registering a UK company and provide services for annual filings. The cost will be:
- UK Company Registration : GBP 50
- Registered Address Service : GBP 25 (for the 1st year)
To place the order please go to
http://www.formationshouse.com/company_formation.htm
and complete the order by providing company name to be registered, select additional services required, provide shares, director and shareholder information and select the payment mode.
For UK company minimum requirement is to have 1 director and must be a person. For other director/s and shareholder/s you can appoint person or corporate entity. In your case you will need to appoint 1 person as company director and for shareholder you can appoint your offshore company.
Registered Address service will allow you to use our address as registered address for your UK company. Please note that service is only to fulfill legal requirements of UK company formation and mails only from Companie House and HMRC will be receieved and forwarded to you by email. Therefore you cannot use our address as your general mailing address.
To use our address as your general mailing address, you will need an additional service of UK Virtual Office, InfiniOffice Mail Only GBP 275 per annum. Virtual Office service can be purchased for 3 or 6 or 12 months.
Once we receive the order, payment and due diligence documents, company will be registered within 2-3 working days.
Documents required for due diligence for company formation:
Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial owner, director/s and shareholder/s.
For Natural Person:
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
For Corporate Entity:
1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles)
2. Proof of ID (Valid Passport) and Proof of Address (Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.) of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body.(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.
We can provide you with services for annual filings for a UK company but we do not provide bookkeeping service. Please note that annual filings for a UK company gets due on the anniversay of company registration. For annual filings following are the services.
For Trading Company:
- Annual Returns : GBP 15 + GBP 25 : Online Company Administrator
- Value Accounting (Preparation and Filing of Annual Accounts and Tax Returns) : GBP 300
For Non-Trading Company:
- Annual Returns : GBP 15 + GBP 25 : Online Company Administrator
- Dormant Company Accounts : GBP 75
For Annual Accounts and Tax Returns you will need to provide us with your daily business transactions of the previous year and using the information provided by you we will prepare and file Annual Accounts and Tax Returns.
For Annual Returns filing we provide you with Online Company Administrator service and you have to file the annual returns yourself. Service will allow you view, change and file information for Annual Returns to Companies House. Using the service you can also file certain applications to Companies House for changes in company information during the year.
So the Basic UK company registration cost will be:
- UK Company Registration : GBP 50
- Registered Address Service : GBP 25 (for the 1st year)
Total : GBP 75
Basic Annual Maintenance cost for Trading UK Company:
- Registered Address Renewal : GBP 100 per annum
- Online Company Administrator : GBP 25 per annum
- Annual Returns : GBP 15 per annum
- Value Accounting : GBP 300 per annum
Total : GBP 440 per annum
Basic Annual Maintenance cost for Non-Trading UK Company:
- Registered Address Renewal : GBP 100 per annum
- Online Company Administrator : GBP 25 per annum
- Annual Returns : GBP 15 per annum
- Dormant Company Accounts : GBP 75 per annum
Total : GBP 215 per annum

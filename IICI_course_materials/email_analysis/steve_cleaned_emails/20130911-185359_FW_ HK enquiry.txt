Dear Oliver

 

This would depends on how quick we may receive the original signed documents
from client.

 

Normally, for the changes of directors and secretary and Registered Office,
the relevant Forms need to be filed with the HK Companies Registry within 15
days from the date of change.

 

For the change of Registered Office, the Forms to change the address on the
Business Registration Certificate shall be filed with the HK Business
Registration Office within 30 days from the date of change.

 

For the share transfer forms, these shall be sent to the HK Stamp Office as
on the date of execution of the documents.

 

If the documents are not filed to the above-mentioned Government
Authorities, offence and late penalty may be imposed.

 

Filing and stamping formalities can be completed in 1 to 2 days, provided
that we have received the original signed documents from the client.

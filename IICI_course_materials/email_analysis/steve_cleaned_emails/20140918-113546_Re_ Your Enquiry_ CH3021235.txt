Dear Sir

Re - Bakemart International UK Limited

The above company was removed from the Register, for non compliance of its filing requirements.  If the company is required on the Register, Companies House will require the following documents, with regards to Administrative Restoration:-

1)  RT01 form, with a filing fee of £100.

2)  B.V. Waiver Letter (Information regarding this can be obtained from the B.V. Division of the Treasury Solicitors - www.bonavacantia.gov.uk/output/BVC14-Administrative-Restoration.aspx

3)  Annual Return form for the year ending -

2nd February 2012, with the filing fee of £40.

2nd February 2013, with the filing fee of £40.

2nd February 2014, with the filing fee of £40.

4)  Accounts for the period ending -

28th February 2012.

28th February 2013.

Please note, the Accounts for the period ending 28th February 2014, will be due by 30th November 2014.

Having checked our records, as the above company was struck of the Register, prior to filing the Accounts for 2012, no late filing penalty would been generated, upon its Restoration.

Yours faithfully
Mr A. Owen.
Restoration Department.

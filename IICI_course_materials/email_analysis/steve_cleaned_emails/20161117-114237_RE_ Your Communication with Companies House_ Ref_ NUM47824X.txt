INBESTOR LIMITED – 06307116

 

Thank you for your email concerning the restoration of the above company.

 If you are going to Administratively restore the company to the register Companies House will require the following documents, -
  
 1)  RT01 form, with a filing fee of £100.
  
 2)  B.V. Waiver Letter. Information regarding this can be obtained from the B.V. Division of the Treasury Solicitors website - https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/390209/BVC_14_Form_V15__1_.pdf
  
 3)  Annual Return forms for the years ending  -04/05/2012, 04/05/2013,  04/05/2014, 04/05/2015 & 04/05/2016, each requiring a £40 filing  fee.
  
 4)  Accounts for the periods ending 31/07/2011, 31/07/2012, 31/07/2013, 31/07/2014 & 31/07/2015
  
 Having checked our records, there are outstanding late filing penalties  of £1500 due on the 2008 accounts, £1500  due on the 2011 accounts and  £1500 (double penalty) on the 2012 accounts

 Forms are available to download from Companies house website, and your  application should be sent for the attention of the restoration section  at Companies House.

 

Please  note cheques drawn on the dissolved company bank account are not  acceptable. Cheques can be drawn on an alternative account, or by  credit/debit  card.

 

Yours faithfully

 

Linda Simmons

Companies House

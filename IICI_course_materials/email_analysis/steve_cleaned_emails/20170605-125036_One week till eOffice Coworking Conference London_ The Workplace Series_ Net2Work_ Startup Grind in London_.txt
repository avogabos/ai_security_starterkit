NET2WORK
The new platform for students looking to start their journey and find the ideal work experience
NET2WORK
is the new interactive platform for young people aged 15 to 26 from all backgrounds and at all stages of education which connects them directly with companies which have opportunities with work experience, internships, apprenticeships and jobs in their chosen career industries.
The platform aims to increase diversity and opportunities providing a place for companies to create brand awareness; by sharing their message, company values, and potential career pathways to young and eager candidates.
Net2Work has large sections dedicated to different industry sectors ranging from business, media, technology, hospitality, engineering, public sector, non-profit, sports, fashion, medical, and the arts.

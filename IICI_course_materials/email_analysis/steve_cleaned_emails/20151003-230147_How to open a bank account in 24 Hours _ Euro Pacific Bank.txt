    Fast Account Opening   
 Once a client has been Pre-Approved and we have received all the client's documents the account is typically opened in 1-3 days   
 Remember to check the following      
        
   Passports & IDs   
 Check Passports and IDs for expiry date, signature and photo            
        
   Utility Bills   
 Verify that the name on the utility bill or other proof of address matches the client's name            
        
   Bank Statements   
 Ensure the client has a bank statement older than 6 months (or a bank reference letter)            
        
   Bank Contract   
 Look through the bank contract to make sure everything is initialed, signed, and that all questions have been answered            
        
   Client's Signature   
 Don't forget the signature on the ID must match the signature on the bank contract            
    By following the above tips & tricks your accounts   
 will receive priority and will be opened as quickly as possible

Owned by one person or more. These people are known as ‘shareholders’. To become a shareholder, you must buy at least one share in the company.
·
Shareholders enjoy limited liability. If the business becomes insolvent, they are only legally required to pay for their shares. The company itself is labile for all debts beyond this sum.

·

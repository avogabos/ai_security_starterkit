UK ready-made companies with bank account have been dormant companies since registration. These companies are not VAT registered.
If you need one the above companies with trading history, then we can submit active accounts for previous year showing minimum transactions on back date. The cost of filing these annual accounts will be GBP 1000.
The minimum requirement for a UK company is:
- VALID UK (England and Wales) postal address for company’s registered office address
- 1 human director
- 1 shareholder (Director can also be a shareholder)
In case if you do not want to provide or cannot provide VALID UK (England and Wales) postal address for company’s registered office address then you choose to use our Registered Office Address service for GBP 25 for the 1st year and from 2nd year onwards cost for the renewal of Registered Address service is GBP 100 per annum.
Please note that Registered Address service allows our client companies to use our address for the purpose of UK company registration and receive mails from Companies House and HMRC only. Company cannot use our address as a general mailing address.
To use our address as company’s general mailing address you can purchase our Virtual Office InfiniOffice Mail Only service for GBP 275 for 12 months.
If required to have VAT registration for the above companies the cost will be as following:
If you can provide company’s actual trading address within UK then VAT registration can be processed for GBP 50 and will take 2-6 weeks to process.
If you cannot provide or do not have company’s actual trading address within UK then VAT registration will be processed for GBP 1000 and will take 4-6 weeks to process. The price GBP 1000 includes:
- VAT Registration
- Virtual Office InfiniOffice Mail Only service for 12 months
- Value Accounting (preparation and filing of annual accounts and tax returns)
- OutSource Accounts Department (preparation and filing of VAT returns)
UK ready-made company will be transferred to you within 2-3 working days after we receive the order, payment and due diligence documents. Also an online account on our website will be created from where you can manage, view and download company documents.
For UK ready-made companies with bank account you have to place an order by sending us an email to
support@formationshouse.com
.
Payment can be made by credit/debit card or bank transfer.
Documents required for due diligence for company formation:
Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial owner/director/s and shareholder/s. Please make sure that scans are clear and in readable size and format.
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature must be visible)
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (date, name, address and account number must be visible)
(Please do not send telephone, mobile, TV or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notarized. And send original and translated copies.

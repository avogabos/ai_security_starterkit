Dear Phil
Thank you for the introduction.
Dear Karel Schrijvers
We can certainly assist you in registering an offshore
company in Seychelles and process bank account for the company with an offshore
bank in St. Vincent Islands. The details are as following:
We have submitted the company name to Seychelles registry
to check the availability, please allow 2 working days for reply on company
name check.
In order to have a SEYCHELLES IBC with Business Bank
Account we offer SEYCHELLES Offshore Starter Pack to our customers. SEYCHELLES
Offshore Starter Pack includes company formation and bank account service.
Seychelles Offshore Starter Pack costs 795 GBP and subsequent
yearly renewal charges for maintenance of Seychelles Company are 400 GBP.
Seychelles Offshore Starter Pack includes:
Seychelles Company Registration
Registered Address
Company Documents
Apostille Set of Company Documents for Bank Account Opening
Bank Account Opening Service
Once the company is formed which will be done in 7-10
working days, it takes 10 working days for the set of the company documents to
be delivered to the address provided by the customer. Please note if you need
documents to be delivered by courier than there will be an additional charge of
65 GBP.
Also for offshore company you are required to provide
your address to show where your company books will be maintained. However, if
you cannot or do not wish to provide your own address, then you can use our
Offshore Compliance Pack for 100 GBP per annum.
Bank Account:
We can have offshore bank accounts for our customers
without any need for them to personally attend the bank procedure.
Bank Account is offered with an offshore bank in
St.Vincent. You will need to maintain minimum USD 500 deposit. The processing
time is 4-8 weeks.
Documents required for bank account opening:
-
Apostille set of company documents
-
Directors detailed CV
-
Business Activity with information about countries from
your will receive and send money.
-
Reference letter from Directors personal bank
-
Proof of ID - Valid Passport (Notarised)
-
Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water
OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.
(Notarised)
You can place the order on this link:
http://www.formationshouse.com/offshore_seychelles.htm
Documents required for due diligence for company
formation:
Scan color copies of following documents are required for
company owner/director/s and shareholder/s.
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water
OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.
If you require further information or assistance you can
contact me at
info@formationshouse.com
or
steve@formationshouse.com
or 0044-(0)207-016-2727 Monday to Friday 11am to 6pm GMT.

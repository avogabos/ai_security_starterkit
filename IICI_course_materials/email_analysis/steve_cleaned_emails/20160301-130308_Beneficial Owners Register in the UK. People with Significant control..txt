** Beneficial Owners Open Register in the UK
------------------------------------------------------------
Is this the end of the UK offshore era? UK is the first EU member state which introduce public Register of the Beneficial Owners. Information on the Companies and LLPs UBOs goes live on Companies House data base as of June 30th 2016.

Read our new article  (http://darwintax.us8.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e90c5dc7917ea0a33feac0e6b&id=00e5029fb4&e=267b4f0486) about People with Significant Control (PSC) in the UK.
We want you to know the important things about Register of People with Significant Control:
* What information on the PSC will be filed as of April 6th 2016
* How the Confirmation Statement will be submitted to the Companies House
* What to do if you do not agree to provide the required information

Dear Steve Smith

company number: 08476526
company name: ARGENTUM CAPITAL PARTNERS

Thank you for your recent e-mail concerning the restoration of the above company.

Please note that the above company was removed from the register of Limited companies for the failure to file statutory documentation, reminders letters were issued on three separate occasions to the company's registered office address and one letter was sent recorded delivery to the listed directors service address, as no response was received to these letters the company was removed from the register on the 28th July 2015

Please note that if the above company is being restored to continue to trade the company can apply for an application for administrative restoration.

A form RT01 is required with an administrative fee of £100 and all outstanding documents- accounts for the period ending 30/04/2014 together with late filing penalties of £1500

The registrar also requires  annual returns for the years 23/04/2015 with filing fees of £40  and a Bona Vacantia waiver letter which can be obtained from the treasury solicitors website- www.gov.uk/bonavacantia

The Bona Vacantia waiver letter, details can be found on our website  www.gov.uk.

Alternatively if the company only requires to restore the company to realise an asset the company can apply to be restored by an order of court, all information on how to restore a company by this procedure can be found on- www.gov.uk.

Please type in company restoration in the contains box which will bring you to a guide to company restoration. Please note that if the company is to be restored to trade all outstanding documents are also required to be filed.

On 10th October 2015 the current version of the form AR01 will be replaced.  If you are unable to file a form AR01 by this date, please download and complete the new version which will be available from our website www.gov.uk/companieshouse on 10th October 2015.

If you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact us

Your faithfully

Mrs R Sollis

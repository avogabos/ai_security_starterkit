Thank you for your interest in our services.
 
 
We can certainly assist you in setting up an offshore company in

Following are the details of our services:
 
 
In order to have a Seychelles IBC with Business Bank Account we
offer Seychelles Offshore Starter Pack to our customers. Seychelles Offshore
Starter Pack includes company formation and bank account service.
 
 
Seychelles Offshore Starter Pack costs 795 GBP and subsequent
yearly renewal charges for maintenance of Seychelles Company are 400 GBP.
 
 
Incorporation of an IBC in Seychelles provides the customers the
following benefits: 
 
 
Seychelles company formation package includes:
 
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Articles of association
- Memorandum of association
- Share certificates and share register
- Resolution of the subscribers appointing the initial directors
- Local Agent
- Registered address
- Apostille company documents for bank account opening
 
 
Bank Account Opening:
 
 
We can have offshore bank accounts for our customers without any
need for them to personally attend the bank procedure.
 
Offshore bank account is offered with FBME, Cyprus (https://www.fbme.com/).
 
Once the account is opened, you will be provided with the account
number after which you can apply for Credit card, Debit card, ATM and/or
internet banking services. Bank account can be opened in the following
currencies:

Euros 
Pound Sterling 
US Dollars 
 
Please find listed below the relevant information for signatory/s,
we will need from you for business bank account with FBME, Cyprus:
Please note that documents must not be notorised.
- Apostille company documents
- Color Copy of passport (must be Valid)- 
- Color copy of Address proof dated within 3 months (Utility bill or bank
statement). If it is not in English then you must also provide us a
professional Translated copy.
- Personal Reference Letter from your bank (original).
 
If the required documents are not in English, you will need to
have these translated and then sent to Formations House. If unable to provide
one of the above mentioned proofs, you can also use a letter from your bank or
a solicitor, or a council tax bill (UK residents only).
 
If you wish to place the order you can simply log on to
http://www.formationshouse.com/offshore_seychelles.htm
 
Documents required for due diligence for offshore company
formation:
Scan copies of following
documents are required for company owner/director/s.

If company director a Natural Person:
1.Proof of ID - Valid Passport (Notarized)
2.Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR
electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (Notarized)
 
If company director a Corporate
Entity:
1.Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of
Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles) (Notarized)
. 
2.Proof of ID and Proof of Address of the beneficial owner of the
Corporate body (Notarized)
 
If documents are not in
English, then you are required to have them translated and notarized. We would
need the original and translated copies.

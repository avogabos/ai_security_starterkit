ANGLO SECURITIES LTD-05226271

Thank you for your e mail, if the company is being restored to remain
on the register, you may apply for administrative restoration,
we would require the following documents and fees:

RT01 form and application fee of £100

The Bona Vacantia waiver letter, details can be found on the Treasury Solicitors
website www.bonavacantia.gov.uk,/output/bvc14-administrative-restoration.aspx<http://www.bonavacantia.gov.uk,/output/bvc14-administrative-restoration.aspx>

Annual Accounts for period end 30/09/2010 and late filing penalty of £1500
Annual Accounts for period end 30/09/2011

Annual Returns (Form AR01) for period end 02/11/2011and filing fee of £40.

Please note the Annual Returns forms can be downloaded from our website www.companieshouse.gov.uk<http://www.companieshouse.gov.uk/>, there are 2 different AR01 forms, if you require any assistance, to ensure the correct form is printed,  please telephone our contact centre on 0303 1234500.

Please note the Annual Return form for the year ending 02/11/2012, will be due by 30/11/12 if the company is not restored by this date this document will also be required with £40 fee.

We cannot accept a cheque drawn on the company account, as when the company
is dissolved, the company bank account is frozen.

All the required documents and fees should be sent in together, and marked for the attention of the Restoration Section (RT01).

Yours faithfully
P Fitzsimmons
Restoration Section
Companies House

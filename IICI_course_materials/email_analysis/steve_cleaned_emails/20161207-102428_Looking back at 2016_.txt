** Let's see what happened in 2016!
------------------------------------------------------------

Here are some highlights of  this year, that have already changed the world, which we knew before:
* Panama scandal over the leak of Beneficial Owners information in a law firm Mossack Fonseca influenced the work of banks all over the world.
* Important changes have come into effect in the BVI: companies are now required to file Registers of the Directors - read our article. (http://darwintax.us8.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e90c5dc7917ea0a33feac0e6b&id=551c758f21&e=267b4f0486)
* Seychelles new International Business Companies Act came in power as of December 1st 2016 - read the full article. (http://darwintax.us8.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e90c5dc7917ea0a33feac0e6b&id=2808e26c3b&e=267b4f0486)
* Common Reporting Standard (CRS) turned the world upside down and proclaimed the bank secrecy officially dead - read about CRS in our blog. (http://darwintax.us8.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e90c5dc7917ea0a33feac0e6b&id=6e7f99d885&e=267b4f0486)
* The United Kingdom has started online register of Beneficial Owners of UK Limited companies and LLPs - read more about PSC register. (http://darwintax.us8.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e90c5dc7917ea0a33feac0e6b&id=25a5e0c1ae&e=267b4f0486)
* Nearly all Latvian banks were audited by the Financial and Capital Markets Commission (FCMC). The results have major impact to non-resident companies sector.

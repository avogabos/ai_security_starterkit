certainly assist you in  filing company dissolution application. Please confirm

The cost to
process Dormant Company Dissolution is GBP 75/-.
Please note
that from the day we file company dissolution application it takes 6 months for
company to be dissolved, during the period company is marked as ‘Active-
Proposal to strike-off’. And as it takes few months to complete dissolution
process, therefore your company will be using our Registered Address during the
period and if for any reason government sends your company any mail on

due. Otherwise you then need to do the filing and then file company
dissolution.

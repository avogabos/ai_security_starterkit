Dear Ellen
We register your company once we receive the payment. Company registration takes 2-3 working days. And once the company is registered you can then download the company documents from your account at our website.
To file Annual Returns you will need to purchase our Online Company Administrator service. Following will be the cost for annual filing of Annual Returns (AR01) and DCA.
Online Company Administrator 25 GBP (per annum)
Annual Returns Government Filing Fee 15 GBP
DCA 75 GBP
Initially we can give you 10% discount for 15 company formation orders. After 15 orders we will give you 15% discount. But please make sure that you must purchase the following services:
-
Company Formation
-
Registered Address
-
Online Company Administrator
-
DCA Filing
Using Online Company Administrator service you can file Annual Returns and can also file following changes:
- Modification of personnel information
- Change of Registered Office Address
- Appointment of Director or Secretary
- Termination of Appointment of Director or Secretary
- Change of details of Director or Secretary
- Notice of increase in nominal capital of company
- Issue new shares
We can also assist you for Company Name Change and Company Cancellation.
Company Name Change price is 25 GBP and it is done within 10-12 working days after we receive the payment.
Company Cancellation for Dormant Companies costs 75 GBP and process takes 3 months.
We can also assist you in registering companies in Seychelles, British Virgin Islands and Delaware USA.
Let me know if you need more information.
Thank you
Best regards,

Dear Raadwane BELKOUCHE,
Thank you for your interest in our services.
The cost to register a LLP company in the UK is GBP 600/- and annual renewal GBP 180/-; the time required to complete the procedure is 3-4 weeks.
The company package includes:
- Company formation fee
- Hard copy Certificate of Incorporation
- The Partnership Agreement after your LLP is formed
- Online PDF versions of company documents
The two basic requirements to form a UK LLP are:
1. A registered address in the UK (i.e. England, Scotland or Wales)
2. Minimum two partners required.
Registered address service:
This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you can not provide a valid UK postal address; the cost for this service is GBP 60/- for first year and from second year onwards renewal charges are GBP 120/-
Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving official mail coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to the sender.
Documents required for due diligence for UK LLP formation:
Scan color copies of following documents are required for company director/s and shareholder/s and authorize contact person. Please make sure that scans are clear and in readable size and format.
If Human Entity:
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature must be visible)
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (date, name, address and account number must be visible)
(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
If Corporate Entity:
1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles)
2. Proof of ID (Valid Passport) and Proof of Address (Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.) of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body.(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.

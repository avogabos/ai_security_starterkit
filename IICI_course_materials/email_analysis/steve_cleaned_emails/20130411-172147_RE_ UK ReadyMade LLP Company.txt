We can certainly provide you with UK ReadyMade LLP company.
Following are the details:
UK ReadyMade LLP Company:
You can view the list of our UK ReadyMade LLP Companies on the following link. It will give you the company name, registration date and price.
https://www.o-c.com/search/formations/readymade.php?ukllp
The company package includes:

- Company
- Hard copy Certificate of Incorporation
- The Partnership Agreement
- Online PDF versions of company documents


The two basic requirements to form a UK LLP are:

1. A registered address in the UK (i.e. England, Scotland or Wales)
2. Minimum two partners required.


Required Details:

Following details are required for LLP incorporation:

- Registered office address i.e. any valid postal address in the UK
- Names and addresses of members of the LLP
- Specification that whether some of all of the members are to be designated members

Registered address service:

This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you cannot provide a valid UK postal address; the cost for this service is 25 GBP.

Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving official mail coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to the sender.
Documents required for due diligence:
Scan copies of following documents are required for company owner/director/s.
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.
To place an order for UK ReadyMade LLP company please go to the following link:
https://www.o-c.com/search/formations/readymade.php?ukllp
On this link you view list and order our Ready Made companies using the 'Purchase' link of the required company name. This link will take you to the 1st step of our 7 step online company formation procedure. On the 2nd step of this procedure you can choose additional services such as Registered Address, Bank Account, etc.
Payment can be made by using any of the following methods:
* Cheque, Draft
* Bank Transfer
* Credit, Debit, Solo & Switch Card
Please include with your payment, your order number, your name, Name of your company, and name(s) of the service(s) for which you are paying.
I hope above information is satisfactory.

Company name 'SILVER CIRCLE ENTERPRIZES LIMITED' is provisionally available for registration in United Kingdom.
To place an order for UK company formation, please click on the link
http://www.formationshouse.com/company_formation.htm
. This link will take you to the 1st step of our 7 step online company formation procedure. On the 2nd step of this procedure you can choose additional services.
Please make sure while placing the order you shoud select 'UK Local Company GBP 50' option after company name search process.
Company registration will be processed within 2 wokring days after we receive the order, payment and due diligence documents.
Documents required for due diligence for company formation:
Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial owner/director/s and shareholder/s. Please make sure that scans are clear and in readable size and format.
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature must be visible)
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (date, name, address and account number must be visible)
(Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.
After placing the order you can email the above mentioned documents to
support@formationshouse.com
. Please do mention your Customer ID and Order Number in the email.

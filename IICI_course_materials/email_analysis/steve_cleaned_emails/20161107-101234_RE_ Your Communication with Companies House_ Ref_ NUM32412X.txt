EUROPEAN TRAVEL SERVICES INT. LIMITED – 08462825

 

 Thank you for your e mail concerning the above company.  The company was  struck off and dissolved due to non filing of statutory documents.   Reminder letter was issued to the register office on 16/12/2013.

  

 If the company is being restored to remain on the register, you may apply for administrative restoration,
 we would require the following documents and fees:  
  
 RT01 form and application fee of £100
  
 The Bona Vacantia waiver letter which costs £64, details can be found on the Treasury Solicitors
 website https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/bona-vacantia

  

 Form AP01 to appoint a director 

 

Annual Accounts for period end 31/03/2014

Annual Accounts for period end 31/03/2015

 
 Annual Returns (Form AR01) for period end 26/03/2014 and filing fee of £40.

Annual Returns (Form AR01) for period end 26/03/2015 and filing fee of £40. 
 Annual Returns (Form AR01) for period end 26/03/2016 and filing fee of £40.
  
 Annual Returns forms can be downloaded from our website  www.companieshouse.gov.uk. 

Please note accounts for period end 31/03/2016 are  due to be filed by 31/12/2016, if the company is not restored by this  due date, these accounts will be required to be filed as part of the  restoration application. 

 We cannot accept a cheque drawn on the company account, as when the company
 is dissolved, the company bank account is frozen.  

 

All the required documents and fees should be sent in together, and marked for the attention of the Restoration Section (RT01).
  
 Yours faithfully 

 

Linda Winters 

Restoration Section
 Companies House

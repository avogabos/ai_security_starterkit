Thank you for your interest in our services.
We can certainly assist you in registering a UK company and provide Registered Address service which will be a UK postal address to be used as company's registered. However please note that service will only used for company registration and only mails from Companies House and HMRC (government departments) will received and forwarded to you email as scan copies. You cannot use Registered Address service for company's general mailing address. If you wish to use our address as company's general mailing address then you will require additional service of Virtual Office Mail Only.
The details for UK company formation with Registered Address are as following:
UK Company Formation:
The cost to register a private limited company in the UK is 50 GBP; the time required to complete the procedure is 3-4 working days.
However, if you choose our Express formation service for additional 25 GBP, your company will be formed within 8 working hours (our working hours are 0900-1700 GMT).
The company package includes:
- Company formation fee
- Domain name registration
- 1 free email address
- Preparation of Memorandum and Articles
- Hard copy of certificate of incorporation
- Online account to manage your company register (company documents)
The basic company requirements are as follows:
• 1 Company Director (can be of any nationality and can reside anywhere in the world)
• 1 Shareholder (Director can also be a Shareholder)
• Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address in England, Wales or Scotland).
To place an order for company formation, please click on the link
http://www.formationshouse.com/company_formation.htm
. This link will take you to the 1st step of our 7 step online company formation procedure. On the 2nd step of this procedure you can choose additional services.
Documents required for due diligence:
Scan copies of following documents are required for company beneficial owner/director/s and shareholder/s.
1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (Please do not send telephone, mobile, tv or internet bill, these are not acceptable.)
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.
Registered address service:
This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you cannot provide a valid UK postal address; the cost for this service is 25 GBP for 1st year.
Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving official mail coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to the sender.
To place an order for this service, please click on the option available on the 2nd step of online ordering procedure.

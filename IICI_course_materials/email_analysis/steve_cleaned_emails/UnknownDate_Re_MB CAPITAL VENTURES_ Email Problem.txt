-Select the option "Add New Email Account" and click "Next"
-Select "POP3" and click "Next"
-Enter your "Email login details" and the name servers as given below:
Incoming Mail Server: pop.yourdomainname
Outgoing Mail Server: smtp.Your Internet Service Provider's Domain Name

Dear Sir/Madam

ZVL EUROPE LIMITED - 05825280

Thank you for your e mail concerning the restoration of the above company.

If you are going to administratively restore the company to the register Companies House will require the following documents, -

1)  RT01 form, with a filing fee of £100.

2)  B.V. Waiver Letter (Information regarding this can be obtained from the B.V. Division of the Treasury Solicitors - www.bonavacantia.gov.uk/output/BVC14-Administrative-Restoration.aspx<http://www.bonavacantia.gov.uk/output/BVC14-Administrative-Restoration.aspx>

3)  Annual Return form for the year ending 18/06/2011 and 18/06/2012 along with the total filing fee of £80.

4)  Accounts for the period ending 31/05/2011.

If you require any further information please contact the restoration department

Yours faithfully



Mrs K L Lark

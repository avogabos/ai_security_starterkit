Hello Irene
Please note that reminder is for filing Annual Accounts, not for Confirmation Statement (formerly called Annual Return).
You need to file Annual Accounts and Tax Returns for period ending on 31-January-2017 before. If company was dormant during the period then you can file Dormant Company Accounts.
If you need our services for the filing then please place order and make payment 15 before the filing date. The cost for service is:
-
For trading companies : Values Accounting : GBP 300
-
For non-trading (dormant) companies : Dormant Company Accounts : GBP 75
If order and payment is delayed then there will be additional charges.
If you require further information or assistance you can contact us by email, by phone or by live chat on our website.

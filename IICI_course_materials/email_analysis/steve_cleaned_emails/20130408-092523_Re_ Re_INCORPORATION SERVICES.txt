Dear Edward Porter


Thank you for your interest in our services.


We can certainly provide you offshore company registration in British Virgin Islands.


The cost of the service is 1300 GBP and it takes10-14 working days for the company to be formed. You would need to renew the company's registration every year. The cost for the annual renewal is 750 GBP.


BVI company formation package includes:

- Certificate of Incorporation
- Articles of association
- Memorandum of association
- Share certificates and share register
- Resolution of the subscribers appointing the initial directors
- Local Agent in BVI 
- Registered address in BVI 


Once the company is formed, it takes 5-6 working days for the set of the company documents to be delivered to the address provided by the customer


Documents required for due diligence:

Scan copies of following documents are required for company owner/director/s.

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.


If you wish to place the order for the company formation service, they simply need to follow the process: 

. Click on the button marked "Order Here" on our website www.o-c.com
. Follow the 7-step online procedure by filling in the required detail
. Purchase on the second step the desired additional services such as Nominee Shareholder, bank account opening etc... 
. Check out at the 7th step after choosing the preferred modes of payment



I hope above information is satisfactory.


If you require further information or assistance you can contact me at info@formationshouse.com or steve@formationshouse.com or 0044-(0)207-016-2727 Monday to Friday 12pm to 9pm GMT.

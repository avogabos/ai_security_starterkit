- UK Private Limited Company by Shares 50 GBP with Registered Address 25 GBP, processing time 2-3 working days.

- UK Private Limited Company by Guarantee 150 GBP with Registered Address 25 GBP, processing time 2-3 working days.


Basic requirements for above are:

* 1 Company Director must be a Natural Person (can be of any nationality and can reside anywhere in the world)
* 1 Shareholder (Director can also be a Shareholder)
* Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address in England, Wales or Scotland).


- UK Limited Liability Partnership 500 GBP with Registered Address 25 GBP, processing time 7-10 working days.

Basic requirements for above are:

* 2 Partners, can be natural persons or corporate entities (can be of any nationality and can reside anywhere in the world)
* Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address in England, Wales or Scotland).


Documents required for due diligence UK for company formation:

Scan color copies of following documents are required for company owner/director/s.


If company director a Natural Person:

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport
2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.


If company director a Corporate Entity:

1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles)
2. Proof of ID (Valid Passport) and Proof of Address (Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement.) of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body.


If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.

- Offshore IBC registration in BVI 590 GBP, processing time 7-10 working days.

- Offshore IBC registration in Belize 390 GBP, processing time 7-10 working days.

- Offshore IBC registration in Hong Kong 595 GBP, processing time 7-10 working days.

- Offshore IBC registration in Gambia 195 GBP, processing time 2-3 working days.


Documents required for due diligence for offshore company formation:

Scan copies of following documents are required for company owner/director/s.

If company director a Natural Person:

1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (Notarized within last 3 months)

2. Proof of Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (Notarized within last 3 months)


If company director a Corporate Entity:

1. Company Documents presenting beneficial owner name (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles) (Notarized within last 3 months).

2. Proof of ID and Proof of Address of the beneficial owner of the Corporate body (Notarized within last 3 months)


If documents are not in English, then you are required to have them translated and notarized.

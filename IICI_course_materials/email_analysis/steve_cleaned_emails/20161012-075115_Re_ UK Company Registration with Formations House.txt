UK Company Formation:



The cost to register a private limited company in the UK is 50 GBP; the time required to complete the procedure is 3-4 working days.



However, if you choose our Express formation service for additional 25 GBP, your company will be formed within 8 working hours (our working hours are 0900-1700 GMT).



The company package includes:



- Company formation fee

- Domain name registration

- 1 free email address

- Preparation of Memorandum and Articles

- Hard copy of certificate of incorporation

- Online account to manage your company register (company documents)



The minimum company requirements are as follows:



• 1 Human Director (can be of any nationality and can reside anywhere in the world)

• 1 Shareholder (Director can also be a Shareholder)

• Registered address in the UK (i.e. a valid postal address in England, Wales or Scotland).



To place an order for company formation:



- Go to http://www.formationshouse.com/ukcompany.php

- Click on 'Order Now' button
- Provide company name to be registered
- Select any additional services if required
- Provide your authorize contact information and to create Customer ID
- Provide information on company director/s and shareholder/s
- Verify given information and provide payment details to complete the order.



Once we have the order, payment and due diligence documents, company registration will complete in 2 working days.





Documents required for due diligence for company formation:



UK regulations require us to establish the identity of all customers that we are providing services to therefore we will need following documents from you and these will be held in our records only and these documents will be not be presented to company registrar.



Scan color copies of following documents are required for company beneficial owner/director/s and shareholder/s.



Please make sure that scans are clear and in readable size and format.



1. Proof of ID - Valid Passport (bearer signature and complete information must be visible)
2. Proof of Residential Address - Last 3 months utility bills (water OR gas OR electricity) or latest personal bank/credit card statement. (issue date, name, address and account number must be visible).



If above documents are not in English then we will need officially translated notarized copies of documents with the originals.



Please do not send telephone, mobile, TV, internet bill or any other document. If documents are not according to above mentioned instructions then it will not be accepted.





Registered address service:



This service can be used to register a company in the UK if you cannot provide a valid UK postal address; the cost for this service is 25 GBP.



Please note, that this address will only be used for receiving official mail coming from Companies House; everything else will be returned to the sender.



To place an order for this service, please click on the option available on the 2nd step of online ordering procedure.





UK Virtual Office Service:

Our Virtual London Office Service is the best deal you will find. We offer unique benefits that no other service provider offers.

We provide you with:

• Postal Mail forwarded to anywhere in world
• Live Phone Answering
• Fax to email
• Call Diversion to anywhere in world

InfiniOffice Mail Only:

InfiniOffice Mail Only can be ordered for a minimum time period of 3 months. The package offers 10 % and 25 % discounts on advance purchases for 6 and 12 months respectively.

This package is offered for a set-up fee of £ 50 GBP and £ 25 GBP per month.

Cost includes the following:

• Mailing address within UK

InfiniOffice 500 (Phone and Fax):

InfiniOffice 500 package can be ordered for a minimum time period of 3 months. It offers a 10 % and 25 % discount on advance purchases for 6 and 12 months respectively.

This package costs £ 25 per month plus one-time set-up fees of £50.

The following services are included:

• A UK phone number
• A UK fax number
• Free calls to 60 locations worldwide
• *100 calls (per month) answered by VO operators free of cost. Afterwards calls will be charged at the rate of £ 0.50 per call
• 10 hours free conference for up to 6 persons per month per company, after it will be charged at £ 0.01 per minute
• If the number of persons is greater than 6 for a conference or allotted free hours ( mentioned above ) is utilized, then addition amount will be deducted from the wallet
• Conference costing is based on (£0.01 per minutes per USER)
• No fax forwarding on fax number (just email or IO)
• Phone rates according to call tariff
• Phone forwarding will be billed according to call tariff

*Any additional calls can be answered by VO operators at nominal charges.

Following features are identical for InfiniOffice Mail Only and InfiniOffice 500:

• 10 GB storage space

• Wallet reminders will be sent when wallet is below £3 and another mail will be sent when wallet hits £0.

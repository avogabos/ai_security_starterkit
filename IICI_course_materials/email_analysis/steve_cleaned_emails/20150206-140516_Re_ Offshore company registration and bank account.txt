Hi,
Thank you for your interest in our services, as per our conversation on phone following are the details for offshore company registration with bank account.
Please note that we can provide you company formation in BVI and Seychelles. The details are as follows:
-
BVI COMPANY with bank account : The cost of the service is GBP 1500
-
SEYCHELLES COMPANY with bank account : The cost of the service is GBP 795
The following services will be included in the above mentioned packs:
- Incorporation of Offshore Company
- Registered Office Address
- Local Registered Agents
- Complete Set of Company Documents (Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum &Articles of Association)
- Apostille set of company documents for bank account processing.
It takes 7 - 10 working days for the Offshore Company to be formed after we will receive the Notarized identification documents from you and the signed company incorporation form that we will provide.
Once the company is formed, it takes 10 working days for the set of the company documents to be delivered to the address provided by the customer. You will be required to provide us the NAME of the company, business plan (Source of initial funds, Proposed business to be undertake & Estimated annual turnover).
IDENTIFICATION DOCUMENTS:
1. Proof of ID (copy of passport)
2. Proof of Residence (i.e. any letter from bank containing the name & address or a copy of your bank statement dated within the last 3 months)
If the documents are not in English then you need to have them translated and notorized. And send original and translated copies.
*The above items can be sent as a scanned copy by email first and a Certified copy of the documents must be provided to us via post for processing the company.
Bank Account processing can done with Rietumu Banka – Latvia or Euro Pacific Bank – St.Vincent Island. Please note it takes 4 weeks to 8 weeks to process the bank account. To have bank account with Rietumu Banka company director/s must be resident within EU states otherwise you can choose to have bank account with Euro Pacific Bank.
I hope above information is satisfactory. If you require further information or assistance, please feel free to E-mail me directly on
steve@formationshouse.com
Looking forward to hear from you.

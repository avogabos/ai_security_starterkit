 Thank you for your e mail, the company was struck off and dissolved due to non- filing of a statutory document.  Reminder letters were issued to the register office address on 3/11/2005, 21/11/2015and director’s address on 21/11/2015.

 If the company is being restored to remain on the register, you may apply for administrative restoration,we would require the following documents and fees:  

 RT01 form and application fee of £100

 The Bona Vacantia waiver letter which costs £64, details can be found on the Treasury Solicitors website https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/bona-vacantia Annual Accounts for period end 28/02/2016

 Annual Returns (Form AR01) for period end 28/09/2015 and filing fee of £40.

Confirmation Statements Part 1-5 (Form CS01) for period end 28/09/2016 and filing fee of £40.  

Please note the Annual Returns/Confirmation Statements forms can be downloaded from our website
 www.companieshouse.gov.uk.
 
 We cannot accept a cheque drawn on the company account, as when the company is dissolved, the company bank account is frozen. 

All the required documents and fees should be sent in together, and marked for the attention of the Restoration Section (RT01).

 Yours faithfully 

 

Linda Winters

Following the conversion of thousands of companies to 'LTD' format, the fallout 
from the Companies Act, 2014 continues with CRO now having processed automatic changes
to company names for certain entities.
The names of the following company types have changed either by their own application
OR by the deeming provision of CA2014:

* CLG - Company Limited by Guarantee
* DAC - Designated Activity Company limited by shares
* DAC - Designated Activity Companies limited by guarantee
* PUC - Public Unlimited Company
* PULC - Public Unlimited Company with no share capital
* ULC - Private Unlimited Company
As such these companies now need to replace their company seals and as quickly as
possible.
Well, help is at hand in the form of CFI's long standing online system for ordering
company seals.
Simply click on the link below (or HERE [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?f=0010WZAjFfwZHzDPzC2oFUK15-pBnF2WK69LO0Wc4sE2-eeMTYUoWDtLJI0XvyUMwb9zKN5MW7V40_Pro0D5vdLYihk8iAOHknzl2_zuu8j17XZjJ1mE6c_6B9YjPCvs5rZb_UYWKBzKrZBTvmWd5Y4zNzK1pI5S1SSJOIbWvjI-tJvFDN4TwWlEVIG9HOyJ9-r-E8KlqH58EI=&c=9D7ETOXRyONynCfDK63tIBZ-M5SBvHHfVOyNwtEE113QBKJ1WZeHow==&ch=HeFhB_1AHJ_ExXWoFV0UJHGpVj5o5iu_mlv8oLHooHvqEUyBBgMc8g==])

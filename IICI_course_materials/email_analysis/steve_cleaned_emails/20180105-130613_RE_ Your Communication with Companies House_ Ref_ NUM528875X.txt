 Dear Sir

  

 Thank you for your e mail, the company was dissolved as the annual return for 19/08/15 was not filed.

  

 If the company is being restored to remain on the register, you may apply for administrative restoration,

 we would require the following documents and fees:  

  

 RT01 form and application fee of £100

 The Bona Vacantia waiver letter, details can be found on the website gov.uk

 please enter waiver letter in the search box for further information.

  

 Annual Accounts for period end 30/11/15, 30/11/16, there are no outstanding penalties.

  

 Annual Returns (Form AR01) for period end 19/08/15 and filing fee of £40.

  

 Please download and complete the new version AR01 (Version 9.0) which is available from our website
 www.gov.uk/companieshouse 

 Please note all the pages of the form must be enclosed even if they are left blank.

  

 CS01 form (Confirmation Statement and Parts 1 - 4) for period end 19/08/16 and filing fee of £40.

 CS01 form (Confirmation Statement) for period end 19/08/17 and filing fee of £40.

  

 Please be aware the requirements regarding disclosure of PSC information have changed with effect from 26 June 2017. Please visit our website for further information  

 www.gov.uk/government/publications/guidance-to-the-people-with-significant-control-requirements-for-companies-and-limited-liability-partnerships

  

 We cannot accept a cheque drawn on the company account, as when a company

 is dissolved, the company bank account is frozen.  

  

 Please allow ten working days from the day of receipt for us to process the documents.

 Please mark all correspondence for the attention of the Restoration Section (RT01).

 Yours faithfully

 Mr D Slee

 Restoration Section

Thank-you for your email regarding: 06894045 ADAM GROUP LIMITED.

To restore the company by Administrative Restoration, the following paper documents and fees are required:-

1)  RT01 form, with a filing fee of £100.

2)  B.V. Waiver Letter which costs £64 (Information regarding this can be obtained from the B.V. Division of the Treasury Solicitors - www.bonavacantia.gov.uk/output/BVC14-Administrative-Restoration.aspx<http://www.bonavacantia.gov.uk/output/BVC14-Administrative-Restoration.aspx>)

3)  Accounts for period ending 31/05/2010, 31/05/2011 and 31/05/12. (Accounts for 31/05/2013 are due by 28/02/2014.)

4)  Annual Return form for the year ending 01/05/2010, 01/05/2011, 01/05/2012 and 01/05/13 and £160 total filing fee.

Having checked our records, there are no outstanding late filing penalties.

    Account Opening   
 Introducing a client to Euro Pacific Bank 
is a simple THREE step process      
        
   Register   
 Fill out an online application form using the client’s email address:  https://europacbank.com/registration/  Register Here [ https://europacbank.com/registration/ ]                
        
   Complete   
 Your client completes the appropriate pre-approval form:  https://files.europacbank.com/docs/account_preapproval_individual.pdf  Personal [ https://files.europacbank.com/docs/account_preapproval_individual.pdf ]or  https://files.europacbank.com/docs/account_preapproval_corporate.pdf  Corporate [ https://files.europacbank.com/docs/account_preapproval_corporate.pdf ]                
        
   Submit   
 Email the pre-approval form, client’s passport, and business plan (if available) to:  mailto:accounts@europacbank.com  accounts@europacbank.com [ mailto:accounts@europacbank.com ]                
    An Account Manager will reply back to you within 
24 hours and guide you through the rest of the process      
    Tips & Tricks   
      
     
Register the account using the client's email address. The client will receive an email with request to complete the account pre-approval form.   
  
To ensure that your accounts are properly recorded, please enter your company's name in the field "How did hear about the bank?"   
  
Upon submitting the pre-approval form to  mailto:accounts@europacbank.com  accounts@europacbank.com [ mailto:accounts@europacbank.com ]you will receive an email with a Ticket ID .

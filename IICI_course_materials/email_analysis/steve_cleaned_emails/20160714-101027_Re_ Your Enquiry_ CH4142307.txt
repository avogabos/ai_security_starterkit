Thank you for your email.

To make an administrative restoration application you will need to file the following documents and fees:

1) Form RT01 with a filing fee of £100.00.

2) The annual returns for 21 January 2016, with a filing fee of £40.00.

3) A waiver letter from the Government Legal Department, which can be ordered online at https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-a-waiver-letter-wa1

Yours sincerely

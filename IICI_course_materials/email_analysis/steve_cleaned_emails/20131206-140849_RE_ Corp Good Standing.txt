In order to revive, Biz Consultant LLC (DE file #4484431) and Biz Services LLC (DE file #4505438), a Certificate of Revival will need to be submitted for each entity to our office with the payment of $200.00 for the filing fee and the payment of the annual franchise taxes due (please contact the Franchise Tax section for the amount due at 302-739-3073 option 3).  For this fee, the request is processed on a routine basis which takes a minimum of 10 to 15 business days to process from the date received.

*        Same Day processing is available for an additional $200.00 and can be faxed in to our office with payment by Visa, Master Card, Discover or American Express.  The request would have to be received by 2 p.m. EST for Same Day Processing and would be sent out either the date of receipt or the next business day depending on the time of completion via regular first class mail unless you provide a Fed Ex or UPS account number for return.
*        24 hour processing is available for an additional $100.00 and can be faxed in to our office with payment by Visa, Master Card, Discover or American Express.  The request would have to be received by 7 p.m. EST and would be sent out either the day after receipt or the next business day depending on the time of completion via regular first class mail unless you provide a Fed Ex or UPS account number for return.

The Certificate is available at the following link:
http://corp.delaware.gov/llcrev09.pdf


A Document Filing Sheet is available at the following link: http://corp.delaware.gov/filingmemo.pdf which can be completed online and printed out to be submitted to our office via fax to 302-739-3812 with payment by one of the credit cards listed


Thank you for your inquiry.

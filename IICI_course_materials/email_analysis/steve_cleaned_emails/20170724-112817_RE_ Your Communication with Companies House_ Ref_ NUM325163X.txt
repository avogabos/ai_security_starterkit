 To restore the company by Administrative Restoration, the following paper documents and fees are required:-

 3)  AR01 form for 20/11/15 and £40 filing fee.

 CS01 form for 20/11/16 and £40 filing fee. Please also complete Part 2. Please see link to guidance for Confirmation Statement which maybe useful: https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/companies-house-guidance-for-limited-companies-partnerships-and-other-company-types#confirmation-statement

 4)  Accounts for the periods ending 30/11/15.

 Accounts for 30/11/16 are due for filing by 31/8/17.

 Payment cannot be drawn on the dissolved company account.

 Please allow 10 working days from receipt of your paper forms to allow us to process your application.
